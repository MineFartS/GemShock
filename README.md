# [GemShock](https://www.roblox.com/games/77084123123071/GemShock)

### A unique take on Battle Royale

---

#### Game Modes
- Free For All
- Teams
- Free Play

---

#### Gameplay
- Earn Gems by surviving rounds
- Lose Gems by Dying
- Use Gems to buy Weapons and tools
- Avoid Rising Lava
- Avoid Storm Clouds
- Drive and Fly Vehicles
- Sprint Around
